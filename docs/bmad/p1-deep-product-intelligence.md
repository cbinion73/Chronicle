# P1 Backlog - Deep Product Intelligence

P1 is about depth.

Once P0 makes Chronicle trustworthy, P1 makes it spiritually and intellectually strong.

---

## Epic P1.1 - Bible Interpretation Becomes Meaningful

### Outcome

Chronicle moves from labeling Scripture to helping the user understand it.

### Stories

#### P1.1.1 - Add Meaning / Reflection layer

Acceptance criteria:

- users can access a passage meaning layer from the Bible tab
- the layer addresses:
  - what this means
  - why the author says it here
  - where it fits in God's plan
  - how it applies to life

#### P1.1.2 - Build passage-level theological synthesis

Acceptance criteria:

- commentary, cross references, concordance, and word study are synthesized into one coherent explanation
- the app does not merely restate keywords

#### P1.1.3 - Add canonical thread tracing

Acceptance criteria:

- Chronicle can trace motifs and echoes across Scripture
- users can follow canonical links in a meaningful order

#### P1.1.4 - Improve translation comparison

Acceptance criteria:

- multiple local translations can be compared side by side
- meaningful differences are visible, not just duplicated text blocks

---

## Epic P1.2 - Prayer and Formation Engine

### Outcome

Prayer becomes a first-class response to Scripture and study rather than a neighboring tab.

### Stories

#### P1.2.1 - Turn any passage into a prayer prompt

Acceptance criteria:

- users can move directly from Scripture to prayer
- prompts are grounded in the selected passage

#### P1.2.2 - Turn any passage into reflection prompts

Acceptance criteria:

- users can generate journaling or reflection prompts from a passage
- prompts remain context-aware and pastorally coherent

#### P1.2.3 - Track prayer requests and answered prayer

Acceptance criteria:

- requests can be revisited
- answered prayer can be recorded
- prayer history becomes part of formation memory

#### P1.2.4 - Add recurring rhythms

Acceptance criteria:

- daily and weekly spiritual formation loops are supported
- progress feels lived, not merely logged

#### P1.2.5 - Connect themes, study, and prayer to growth history

Acceptance criteria:

- Chronicle can show patterns over time
- the user can revisit how Scripture and prayer have shaped a season

---

## Epic P1.3 - Chronicle AI Becomes Structured and Useful

### Outcome

Chronicle AI becomes a grounded assistant with specific formation jobs.

### Stories

#### P1.3.1 - Formalize Chronicle AI roles

Acceptance criteria:

- Bible Study Agent is distinct from Prayer Partner
- Discipleship Coach is distinct from Reflection / Writing Guide

#### P1.3.2 - Add structured AI actions

Acceptance criteria:

- users can trigger concrete actions such as:
  - summarize passage
  - compare translations
  - generate study questions
  - turn into prayer
  - extract application

#### P1.3.3 - Improve page context grounding

Acceptance criteria:

- AI actions inherit the active page context reliably
- responses stay tied to the right book/day/passage

#### P1.3.4 - Persist meaningful AI conversations by context

Acceptance criteria:

- conversation history can attach to a passage, day, or book
- AI memory feels useful rather than noisy

---

## Epic P1.4 - Dynamic Study Generation

### Outcome

Imported books become rich study experiences rather than static OCR artifacts.

### Stories

#### P1.4.1 - Generate dynamic study layouts by day and book

Acceptance criteria:

- daily study surfaces reflect the actual structure of the source material
- layouts adapt by content type

#### P1.4.2 - Detect workbook structures automatically

Acceptance criteria:

- numbered prompts, checklists, and writing regions are detected more reliably

#### P1.4.3 - Convert non-devotional books into daily study paths

Acceptance criteria:

- long-form books can be transformed into day-by-day study modules
- source fidelity is preserved

#### P1.4.4 - Keep generated study aligned to source material

Acceptance criteria:

- generated study does not drift away from the underlying source pages
- source and study remain traceable to one another

---

## P1 Recommended Execution Order

1. P1.1.1 Add Meaning / Reflection layer
2. P1.1.2 Build passage-level theological synthesis
3. P1.2.1 Turn any passage into a prayer prompt
4. P1.3.2 Add structured AI actions
5. P1.4.1 Generate dynamic study layouts by day and book

## P1 Definition of Done

- Bible interpretation feels insightful
- prayer is genuinely integrated into the study flow
- Chronicle AI acts like a grounded formation assistant
- imported books feel alive and context-aware
